Hello,

I'm Xiao Han, the designer of this website, you can also call me Suki.

During the period of my college, I took plenty of computer courses. Computer Application made me understand computer, Data Structures enable me to immerse in the programming world, and C Language, C++, Java and Python gave me solid programming skills. Database, Cyber Security, Data Communication and a series of computer courses let me gradually appreciate the infinite charm of the computer world！

This semester, I met a professor who introduced me into a new world: Front-End Web Development. Our CSCI 4970/6970 course is taught by Professor Gao, the most popular teacher in the College of Computer Science. Each of his classes was so attractive that I was thirsty for web knowledge. Under his guidance, I learned to do web pages and write JS code step by step.

In the front-end Web Development of the semester, my favorite assignment was Class Project, and I felt the fun of creation and exploration. I especially love the CSS section, which has a wide range of design styles that interest me. In the process of learning, I gradually found that I was deeply attracted by this course and decide the direction that I was interested in in the future: Front-End Web Development.

As I said on the stage when I won the National Scholarship: “I love computers. It is my window to the world.” The next decade will be the era of big data and artificial intelligence. All industries will benefit from the application of computer skills. I sincerely suggest that you learn more computer knowledge, I believe you will gain a lot!

Welcome to the College of Computer Science!
